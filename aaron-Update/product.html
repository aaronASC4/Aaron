The Brain Buzzer

The Product

The Brain Buzzer is an alarm clock with multiple snooze buttons. It has boxy cube shape, changing color scheme, many inverted snooze buttons located along the sides and back of the alarm clock, a different one being activated every day.

Project Details

  • Varying Colors
  • Free Shipping
  • Easy to use
  • Managable and accesible
